Warehousing Solutions in Goa

CREC is the leading organised provider of warehouses in Goa. We provide customised warehouses from 4000-20000 square metres that are conveniently situated in a cargo terminal in Zuari, close to Mormugao Port, the airport, Verna Industrial Estate and Verna Train Container Depot. Large internal roads ensure organized traffic so there is a free movement of vehicles. The roof is at a 15-metre clear height and with vertical stacking capability, you can efficiently store goods. The warehouse structures are RCC and built to last. There are also security booths and bathrooms at each site.
