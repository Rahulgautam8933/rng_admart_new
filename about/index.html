About Us

Chowgule Real Estate and Construction - CREC

CREC (Chowgule Real Estate and Construction) has expanded from creating beautiful residential and commercial spaces over the past 60 years to the building and management of warehouses, business centers and industrial parks for the local and global community. Our company ethos has been to grow Goa’s economy and to provide for the needs of families rather than oversupplying the real estate market. In our endeavor, we have completed some of the landmark buildings as well as government and naval tenders, factories and institutional projects in the state. Proud to be connected to a heritage of industrial expertise, CREC is now managed by the third generation of the Chowgule family.

Our happy customers trust the quality, value and out-of-the-box thinking that they have come to associate with CREC. We believe that quality is not a fortuitous accident but comes with attention to detail and that little extra effort we make. Our mission is to make the buying of real estate as cost effective as possible while delivering a fine product. Along with good value, our customers experience a sense of security in legal, ethical and investment aspects. This is because of our commitment to providing accurate information, skilled scrutiny and comprehensive real estate advice.

All our projects are in locations that strike a balance between proximity to urban conveniences and closeness to nature. A great deal of care is taken to build in harmony with the environment such as creating green landscape areas instead of cement paved ones. We pride ourselves in taking a unique approach to combining residential and commercial buildings to create a more conducive work environment.

Why Choose CREC?

Empowering Growth, Enhancing Spaces

Our mission is to cultivate vibrant communities and thriving businesses by delivering exceptional real estate solutions. We specialize in creating innovative and sustainable office spaces and warehouses for rent, while also providing high-quality residential properties for sale. Committed to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction, we strive to be a catalyst for success in every space we develop, fostering a dynamic environment where businesses and individuals can flourish.

Transforming Lives, Elevating Spaces

Our vision is to be the industry leader in real estate development, reshaping the urban and commercial landscape with a commitment to excellence. We envision a future where our innovative residential, industrial, and commercial spaces redefine the way people live and work. By offering cutting-edge office and warehouse solutions for rent and delivering distinctive residential properties for sale, we aim to create environments that inspire success, foster growth, and leave a lasting legacy.
