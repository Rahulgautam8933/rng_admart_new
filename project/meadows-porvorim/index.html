Overview

Meadows

Meadows, is an exclusive residential complex situated Paithona, Porvorim, North Goa; in a quiet idyllic setting surrounded by the beautiful countryside. Nestling against the backwaters of river Mandovi serving as a natural backdrop and serenity, Meadows gives you the best of both sides of the world, the experience of an unending blend of nature and fresh air convoyed by a majestic lifestyle set in a scenic location. Meadow complex is centrally located where Panjim City is just 5 Kms and Calangute is around 7 Kms.
Meadows consists of 2 bedroom (175 sq. mtrs.) and a 3 bedroom penthouse (350 sq. mtrs.) apartment.

Location

Meadows, is located at Paithona, Porvorim North Goa, near Nisha’s play school. Meadows is centrally located between Goa’s two major cities, Mapusa and Panaji. Mapusa city is just 6 Kms and Panaji city only 5 Kms way. Also, Goa’s famous beach belt (Candolim to Vagator) is diagonally located just 6 kms away. Paithona village is enclosed by lush green fields and water-bodies and a scenic view that rejuvenate your body and mind.
